## Overview

The TurtleBot 4 by Clearpath Robotics is the industry-standard mobile robot platform for ROS2 development. Built on the iRobot Create 3, it is the go-to platform for learning and teaching ROS2 navigation and perception.

## Use Cases

- ROS2 navigation and SLAM education
- Autonomous mobile robot research
- Multi-robot coordination experiments
- Perception and mapping benchmarks
