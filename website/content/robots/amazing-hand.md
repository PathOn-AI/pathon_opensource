## Overview

The Amazing Hand by Pollen Robotics is a self-contained, 3D-printed dexterous robotic hand. It offers 8 degrees of freedom with four fingers (two motors each) at a cost under €200.

## Use Cases

- Dexterous manipulation research
- Grasping and in-hand manipulation
- Prosthetics prototyping
- Human-robot interaction studies
