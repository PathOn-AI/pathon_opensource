## Overview

The myCobot 280 Pi is a compact 6-DOF collaborative robot arm from Elephant Robotics, powered by a Raspberry Pi 4B. It is designed for education, research, and light industrial applications.

## Use Cases

- ROS2 development and education
- Visual servoing and pick-and-place
- Lightweight manipulation research
