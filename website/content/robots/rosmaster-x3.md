## Overview

The ROSMASTER X3 by Yahboom is a ROS2-compatible mobile robot featuring mecanum wheels for omnidirectional movement. It is a versatile platform for navigation and perception research.

## Use Cases

- Omnidirectional navigation research
- Indoor autonomous delivery prototyping
- SLAM and mapping with various sensor configurations
- Mobile manipulation base platform
