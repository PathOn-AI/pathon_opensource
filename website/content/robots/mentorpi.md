## Overview

The Hiwonder MentorPi is a ROS2 educational mobile robot powered by Raspberry Pi 5. It comes with a rich sensor suite and offers three interchangeable chassis options: Mecanum, Ackermann, and tank treads.

## Use Cases

- ROS2 education and curriculum development
- SLAM and navigation experiments
- Multi-sensor fusion research
- Comparing different locomotion strategies
