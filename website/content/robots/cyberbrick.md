## Overview

CyberBrick by Bambu Lab is a modular hardware kit that brings 3D-printed robots to life. It provides standardized electronic modules that snap into 3D-printed structures, enabling makers to build functional robots without complex wiring.

## Community Projects

The CyberBrick ecosystem on MakerWorld includes robotic arms, combat robots, RC vehicles, walking robots, and custom animatronics.

## Use Cases

- STEM education and maker projects
- Rapid prototyping of robot designs
- Community-driven robot building
- Introduction to robotics for beginners
