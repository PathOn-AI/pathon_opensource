# Privacy Policy for Scanner3D

**Last Updated: April 14, 2026**

**PathOn Robotics** ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Scanner3D mobile application (the "App").

## Data Collection and Use

Scanner3D captures the following sensor data from your device **only during active scanning sessions**:

- **Camera (RGB):** Used to capture color images for 3D scanning.
- **LiDAR / Depth Sensor:** Used to capture depth maps for 3D scanning.
- **Motion Sensors (Accelerometer & Gyroscope):** Used to capture IMU data for pose estimation.
- **Camera Pose (ARKit):** Used to track device position and orientation.
- **Confidence Maps (ARKit):** Used to filter low-confidence depth measurements.

## Data Storage

Scanner3D saves captured scan data (depth, RGB, camera pose, intrinsics, and confidence maps) **locally on your device** in the app's Documents directory. Scans can be exported as zip files via the iOS Files app.

**No data is sent to any remote server.** All data remains on your device unless you choose to share or export it.

## Third-Party Services

Scanner3D **does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services**. No data is sent to external servers.

## Data Sharing

We **do not collect, store, or share** any personal data or sensor data with any third party.

## Children's Privacy

Scanner3D does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App or on our website.
